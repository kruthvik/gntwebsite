Most Famous Artworks

                Pablo Picasso is a renowned artist and if he were still alive today, the wealthiest. His art has been recognized thousands of times. Picasso had had many periods for his artwork. He also had many famous artworks. One of his most notable paintings is “The Old Guitarist”. This painting is mysterious and was painted in Picasso’s blue period when he was in his 20s. Another famous painting of Picasso is ‘Garcon a la Pipe’. This painting is much less obscure because it is held in a private collection. This painting was painted in Picasso’s rose period, the period right after the blue one.

'Les Demoiselles d'Avignon' is another famous painting. This painting is much more well known and has been criticized numerous times for being “immoral”. This painting was the first painting to use cubism, an art form Picasso had invented. This painting took over a decade to paint. ‘Guernica’ is another painting made by Picasso in his later years. This painting is mysterious and vague, but it’s a depiction of an aerial bombing raid.

The ‘Weeping Woman’ is another painting formed on cubism. It was made in the same year as ‘Guernica’ and is also famous. This painting is much deeper and the “sequel” to ‘Guernica’.
