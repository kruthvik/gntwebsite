Similar Artists

                Pablo Picasso is one of the most influential artists in the 20th century. He both inspired, and was inspired by artists. There are many artists similar to them. For example, Picasso was inspired by Salvador Dali for much of his artwork. They both have Spanish roots and have similar artwork. Picasso’s work in surrealism influenced Salvador Dali’s most iconic paintings. Vincent Van Gogh is another mysterious artist with artwork similar to Picasso. He is most notable for “The Starry Night”. Edvard Munch also painted around the time Picasso had. He is most notable for, “The Scream” and has similar surrealism and post expressionism artworks. Paul Gaugin is also similar to Picasso. Gaugin painted much earlier than Picasso, but has similar realism. His paintings are known to be extremely realistic and detailed. Though Picasso focused in much more abstract paintings, some of his work matches Gaugin’s.

  • Vincent Van Gogh
  • Edvard Munch
