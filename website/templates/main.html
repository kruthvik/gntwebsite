Pablo Picasso is one of the most renowned artists in history. He has influenced many famous artists and their pieces. He was not only an artist but was also a Spanish painter, sculptor, printmaker, ceramicist and theatre designer. Though he is extremely famous, he is still mysterious and therefore, a little vague. He is famous for his abstract artworks that display such mystery. Some of his most famous works are “Guernica,” “The Old Guitaris,” and “The Weeping Woman”. The coincidence in his life is how his first word was pencil; this definitely foreshadowed his future.
